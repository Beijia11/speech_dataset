Our Dataset Structure

  1. Talkshow Dataset – GitHub: TalkSHOW
  2. TANGO Dataset (TED Business) – Google Drive
  3. [Self-Collected] TED Dataset – Manually curated TED content with consistent speakers and camera angles.
  4. [Self-Collected] Celebrity Interview Dataset – Interviews featuring expressive gestures and diverse communication styles.
  5. [Self-Collected] Animated Character Dataset – Gesture-rich clips from animated figures such as cartoons and avatars.
